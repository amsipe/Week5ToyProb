The Best Pepperoni Pizza

A guide on how to make a great tasting pepperoni pizza.

Ingredrients:

  • 1 can (8oz) Tomato Sauce - No Salt Added
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 prebaked thin pizza crust (12-inch)
  • 1 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • ⅓ cup sliced pepperoni

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Combine tomato sauce, parmesan cheese, and oregano.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over crust.
  4. Sprinkle pizza with mozzarella cheese. Top with pepperoni.
  5. Place on ungreased pizza pan. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until edges of crust are browned lightly and cheese melts.
  6. Cut pizza into 6 slices.
